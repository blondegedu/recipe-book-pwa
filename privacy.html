🍳 Recipe Book — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

Overview

Recipe Book is a personal recipe organizer. We respect your privacy and collect minimal data necessary to provide the service.

Data We Store

  • Recipes: Your recipes are stored locally on your device using IndexedDB (browser storage). They are not uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly use the Share feature.
  • Shared Recipes: When you share a recipe via the Share link feature, that recipe's content is stored on our server so others can view it. Shared recipes do not include your identity.
  • Settings: Your app preferences (book name) are stored locally on your device.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • No location data
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No cookies for tracking purposes

Recipe Scraping

When you use the "Add from URL" feature, our server fetches the recipe page on your behalf to extract recipe data. We do not store the URLs you scrape or any browsing history.

Third-Party Services

Recipe Book does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Deletion

Since your recipes are stored locally on your device, you can delete them at any time from within the app. Clearing your browser data or uninstalling the app will also remove all local data. Shared recipe links can be removed upon request.

Children's Privacy

Recipe Book does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or personal data entry.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
