## Full Description

Recipe Book is a simple, fast, and private recipe organizer that lives right on your phone. Paste a recipe URL and it's instantly scraped and saved. Or copy recipe text from Facebook, a blog, a friend's message — paste it in and Recipe Book intelligently parses out the ingredients and instructions for you.

YOUR RECIPES, YOUR WAY
• Add recipes from any URL — automatic scraping from thousands of recipe sites
• Quick Paste — copy text from anywhere and the smart parser sorts it out
• Manual entry for family recipes and handwritten favorites
• Organize by category: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Dessert, and more
• Search and sort your collection instantly

SHARE WITH ANYONE
• Share recipes as beautiful standalone HTML files
• Recipients can view and print without installing anything
• Built-in print formatting for clean recipe printouts

PRIVATE & OFFLINE
• All recipes stored locally on your device
• No account required
• No tracking, no ads, no data collection
• Works offline after first load

CLEAN & SIMPLE
• Designed to look like a real recipe book
• Warm, readable typography
• Fast and lightweight — no bloat

Whether you're saving grandma's banana bread or that viral cookie recipe, Recipe Book keeps everything in one place, beautifully organized, and easy to share.
